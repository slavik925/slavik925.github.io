Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Overview

Not Going is an entertainment app for creating pretend mood-based travel plans. It does not process real hotel bookings, payments, reservations, travel purchases, or cancellations.

Information We Collect

Not Going does not require an account and does not ask for payment information.

The app may store pretend trip details locally on your device, such as selected destinations, hotel-style stays, dates, add-ons, and notes shown inside the app.

Tracking And Advertising

Not Going does not track you across apps or websites and does not use third-party advertising identifiers.

Third-Party Services

Some app features may request hotel-style listing, map, image, or link information from third-party services. Those services may receive technical information needed to respond to the request, such as approximate destination search details, device network information, and standard server logs.

External hotel or web links open outside Not Going and are governed by the privacy policies of those third-party websites or services.

Data Storage

Saved pretend trips are stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes locally stored app data from that device.

Children

Not Going is not intended to collect personal information from children.
