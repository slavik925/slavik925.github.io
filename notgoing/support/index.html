About The App

Not Going is an entertainment app for creating pretend mood-based travel plans.

It does not process real hotel bookings, payments, reservations, travel purchases, or cancellations. Hotel-style listings and prices are playful planning content only and are not guaranteed.

Privacy

Not Going does not require an account and does not collect payment information. Any saved pretend trips are stored locally on the device.
